Double Door Home Carrier Services

As the industry leader, SFC Cool Carrier Bareilly have elevated Packing and Moving to a fine art. Catering to the specific needs of our clients we have designed a double door home carrier. The concept enables the sharing of cost; however the space is exclusively and independently allocated to each client.

The customer has the added advantage of using a personal lock, which guarantees that the goods are not interfered with in any way along the journey.
